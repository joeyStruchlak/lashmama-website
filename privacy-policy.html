Privacy Policy

Luxury Lash & Beauty

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lashmama™ ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Purni (ABN holder), collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Lashmama™ mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account information: Full name, email address, phone number, and profile photo.
  • Booking information: Appointment history, service preferences, booking notes, and health intake form responses.
  • Payment information: Payment card details are processed and stored securely by Stripe. We do not store your full card number — we only receive a payment confirmation token.
  • Health information: Allergy disclosures, medical conditions, and consent records submitted via our health intake form. This is sensitive information collected solely for safe service delivery.
  • Device & usage information: Push notification tokens, device type, and app usage data for service improvement.
  • Communications: Messages sent through the in-app messaging feature between you and Lashmama™ staff.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process bookings, deposits, and payments.
  • To send appointment reminders, booking confirmations, and service notifications via push notifications and in-app messages.
  • To deliver safe and appropriate beauty services based on your health information.
  • To manage our VIP loyalty program and eligibility.
  • To improve our app and services through analytics.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

3. Payment Processing — Stripe

All payment processing is handled by Stripe (stripe.com/au/privacy), a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. When you make a payment, your card details are transmitted directly to Stripe and are never stored on our servers. Stripe may collect and process your payment information in accordance with their own privacy policy.

4. Push Notifications

With your consent, we send push notifications for appointment reminders, booking confirmations, promotional updates, and important account alerts. Push notification tokens are stored securely in our database and used solely for this purpose. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings.

5. How We Store Your Information

Your personal information is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure, hosted on servers in accordance with industry-standard security practices. We implement row-level security, encrypted connections, and access controls to protect your data. Health information is stored with additional access restrictions and is accessible only to Lashmama™ staff involved in your care.

6. Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing.
  • Supabase — our database and backend infrastructure provider.
  • Expo / Apple / Google — for push notification delivery.
  • Lashmama™ staff — artists and managers who need your booking and health information to provide services.
  • Legal authorities — where required by Australian law.

7. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at lashmamatm@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention

  • Account and booking information is retained for as long as your account is active and for up to 7 years after closure for legal and tax compliance purposes.
  • Health intake forms are retained for 7 years in accordance with Australian health records legislation.
  • In-app messages are retained indefinitely to support service continuity.
  • Push notification tokens are deleted when you delete your account or revoke notification permissions.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as our Shopify store or Fresha booking). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
