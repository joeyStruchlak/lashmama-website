Purni Ranasinha is an internationally awarded Master Lash Artist and the founder of Lash Mama™, a premium lash salon and academy also celebrated for award-winning hair and makeup artistry. Her journey began at 16 as a beauty pageant winner and television personality, before travelling the world as Qatar Airways cabin crew. Today she is a multi-award-winning educator, having completed more than 200 wedding transformations and trained lash, hair and makeup artists across Adelaide and Melbourne.
